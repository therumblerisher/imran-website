Rent is one of the fastest ways to understand what a city rewards.

You can learn a lot from which neighbourhoods command a premium, what people are willing to tolerate for proximity, and how far young workers stretch to stay inside a certain map of opportunity.

This one will probably become an essay about status, commute pain, and how cities reveal their values through monthly obligations.
